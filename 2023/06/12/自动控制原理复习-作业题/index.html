第二次作业

T1

已知函数\(f(x)\)的拉氏变换为\(F(S)=\frac{10}{S(S+1)}\),试求\(f(t)的终值\)

解：由终值定理可得\(\lim\limits_{t\to \infty}f(t)=\lim\limits_{s\to 0}SF(S)=\lim\limits_{S\to 0}S\frac{10}{S(S+1)}=\lim\limits_{S\to 0}\frac{10}{1+S}=10\)

所以\(f(t)\)的终值为10

T2

试求下列函数的拉氏反变换 (1)\(F(S)=\frac{S+1}{S(S^2+S+1)}\) 解：\(F(S)=\frac{S+1}{S(S^2+S+1)}=\frac{k_1}{S}+\frac{k_2S+k_3}{S^2+S+1}\)

\(k_1=\frac{S+1}{S(S^2+S+1)}S|_{S=0}=1,\quad (1+k_2)S^2+(1+k_3)S+1+=S+1=>k2=-1,k_3=0\)

\(F(S)=\frac{1}{S}-\frac{1}{S^2+S+1}\)

\(\mathscr{L^{-1}}[F(S)]=\mathscr{L^{-1}}[\frac{1}{S}]-\mathscr{L^{-1}}[\frac{1}{S^2+S+1}]=\mathscr{L^{-1}}[\frac{1}{S}]-\mathscr{L^{-1}}[\frac{S+\frac{1}{2}-\frac{1}{2}}{(S+\frac{1}{2})^2+(\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2})^2}]\)

\(=\mathscr{L^{-1}}[\frac{1}{S}]-\mathscr{L^{-1}}[\frac{S+\frac{1}{2}}{(S+\frac{1}{2})^2+(\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2})^2}]+\mathscr{L^{-1}}[\frac{\frac{1}{2}}{(S+\frac{1}{2})^2+(\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2})^2}]\)

\(=\mathscr{L^{-1}}[\frac{1}{S}]-\mathscr{L^{-1}}[\frac{S+\frac{1}{2}}{(S+\frac{1}{2})^2+(\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2})^2}]+\mathscr{L^{-1}}[\frac{1}{\sqrt{3}}\frac{\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}}{(S+\frac{1}{2})^2+(\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2})^2}]\)

\(=1(t)-e^{-\frac{1}{2}t}\cos{\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}t}+\frac{1}{\sqrt{3}}e^{-\frac{1}{2}t}\sin{\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}t}\)
