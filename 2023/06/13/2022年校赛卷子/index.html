单项选择题

T1 (C)

\(f(x)=\int_{0}^{\sin x}{\ln (1+\frac{a}{2}t^2)} dt \sim\int_{0}^{\sin x}\frac{a}{2}t^2dt\sim\frac{a}{6}t^3,\quad g(x)=\tan x -\sin x\sim\frac{1}{2}x^3\)

\(\because f(x)\sim g(x),\quad \therefore\lim\limits_{x\to 0}\frac{f(x)}{g(x)}=\frac{a}{3}=1=>a=3\)

T2(A)

\(f'(0)=\lim\limits_{x\to0}\frac{f(x)-f(0)}{x}=\lim\limits_{x\to0}\frac{f(x)}{x}=\lim\limits_{x\to0}\frac{(e^x-1)(e^{2x}-2)...(e^{nx}-n)}{x}=\lim\limits_{x\to0}(e^{2x}-2)(e^{3x}-3)...(e^{nx}-n)\)

\(=(-1)^{n-1}(n-1)!\)

T3(B)

积分区域关于\(y=x\)对称，所以\(I_1=I_3=0,I_2>0,I_4<0\)

T4(C)

级数\(\sum\limits_{n=2}^{\infty}[\sin \frac{1}{n}-k\ln (1-\frac{1}{n})]\)收敛,所以\(\sin \frac{1}{n}-k\ln (1-\frac{1}{n}) \sim \frac{1}{n^p}\)且\(p>1\)

\(\sin \frac{1}{n} \sim \frac{1}{n}-\frac{1}{6}\frac{1}{n^3}+o(\frac{1}{n^3}),\quad -\ln(1-\frac{1}{n})\sim\frac{1}{n}+\frac{1}{2n^2}+o(\frac{1}{n^2})\)

\(\therefore k=-1\)

T5(D)

\(e^{xz}=x+1+z,\quad e^{xz}z=1+\frac{\partial z}{\partial x}\)

\(\because(x,y)=(0,1),z=0,\frac{\partial z}{\partial x}=-1\)

\(1=y+z\quad0=1+\frac{\partial z}{\partial y},\quad \frac{\partial z}{\partial y}=-1\)

填空题

T6(\(\ln2\))

\(\lim\limits_{n\to\infty}(\frac{1}{n+1}+\frac{1}{n+2}+...+\frac{1}{n+n})=\lim\limits_{n\to\infty}\sum\limits_{i=1}^{n}\frac{1}{n+i}=\lim\limits_{n\to\infty}\frac{1}{n}\sum\limits_{i=1}^{n}\frac{1}{1+\frac{i}{n}}=\int_{0}^{1}\frac{1}{1+x}dx=\ln(1+x)|_{0}^{1}=\ln2\)

T7(3)

\(\lim\limits_{x\to0}e^{\frac{1}{x^2}\ln(\cos x+\frac{f(x)}{x})}=e^{-1},\quad \therefore\lim\limits_{x\to0}\frac{\ln(\cos x+\frac{f(x)}{x})}{x^2}=-1,x\to0,\ln(\cos x+\frac{f(x)}{x})\to0\)

\(\frac{f(x)}{x}\to0,\therefore\lim\limits_{x\to0}\frac{\cos x+\frac{f(x)}{x}-1}{x^2}=\lim\limits_{x\to0}\frac{\cos x-1}{x^2}+\frac{f(x)}{x^3}=\lim\limits_{x\to0}-\frac{1}{2}+\frac{f(x)}{x^3}=-1\)

\(\therefore\alpha=3\)
